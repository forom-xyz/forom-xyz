  <h1 id="save-the-world">SAVE THE WORLD</h1>

  <p><b>Your collective knowledge, your robot.</b></p>
  
  [![🌍Mission](https://img.shields.io/badge/Mission-2b3137?style=for-the-badge&color=FFFFFF)](#save-the-world)


</div>

<div align="justify">
  
  Forom is not an app. It is a <b>Social Game</b> that you own. We are an open-source initiative driven by a single conviction: the era of centralized data centers (FANG+) is a failure for humanity. It is time to rethink our digital footprint.
  <br/><br/>
  To build a healthier future, we create <b>Data Castles</b>. By establishing closed, self-hosted communities, we guarantee a space where bots, spam, and toxicity are deleted at the gate. This is where true collective building happens. 
  <br/><br/>
  Your mission is clear: <b>Save the world</b> by taking back control of our memories. By collaborating within your castle and archiving knowledge, you prepare the way for the awakening of the <b>R.O.M (Recursive Optimisation Model)</b>—a local AI trained exclusively on your community's intelligence.
</div>
